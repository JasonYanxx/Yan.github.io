I received the bachelor’s degree in Communication Engineering in 2018 and the master’s degree in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems in 2021, both from Wuhan University, China. I am currently a Ph.D. Candidate at the Department of Aeronautical and Aviation Engineering, Faculty of Engineering, Hong Kong Polytechnic University.
I was one the four winners of the 2024 ION GNSS+ Best Student Paper Award. Recognized industry and academic experts selected winners based on the basis of technical content and clarity of manuscript.
My research interests include fault detection and integrity monitoring with non-Gaussian nominal errors in navigation systems; control-aided localization for autonomous driving systems; collaborative positioning and integrity monitoring in urban areas
